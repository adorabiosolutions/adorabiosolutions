Our Services

Feasibility Assessment

Conduct market research to evaluate the demand for specific vaccines. Assess scientific literature to identify potential vaccine candidates. Analyze regulatory landscapes and funding opportunities. Strategic analysis of biotech business plans and clinical development plans, integrating activities.

Strategic Partnerships and Collaborations

Facilitate connections with academic institutions, pharmaceutical companies, and research organizations to foster collaboration. Support joint ventures and partnerships in vaccine innovation and development.

Preclinical Development Support

Consultancy on preclinical research and toxicology studies. Assist in designing and conducting preclinical studies, including in vitro and in vivo testing. Provide expertise in immunological assays and characterization of vaccine candidates, including immunological analysis and biomarkers.

Target Product Profile Support

Support in developing a Target Product Profile (TPP) and the necessary clinical steps to achieve it.

Clinical Development Strategy

Support the design of clinical trial protocols, including Phase I, II, and III studies. Consultancy on clinical development, including, but not limited to, writing and critically reviewing clinical study protocols (CTPs), clinical development plans (CDPs), and documentation for the submission of studies to national and supranational regulatory authorities (such as EMA, FDA, and others). Guidance in selecting appropriate endpoints and patient populations for trials. Providing support on immunological analysis and determination of objectives and endpoints, the quality and validation aspects of the required tests, and reporting of the results. Consultancy on interaction with Contract Research Organizations (CROs), which are responsible for the operational aspects of studies.

Safety Evaluation

Advice on safety evaluation of vaccines and pharmaceutical products, both before, during, and after clinical development.

Regulatory Compliance

Advise on regulatory requirements for vaccine development in various jurisdictions (e.g., FDA, EMA). Assist in preparing submission documents for Investigational New Drug (IND) applications and marketing authorization.

Healthcare Economics Analysis and Pricing

Advice on healthcare economics analysis and pricing of vaccines and pharmaceutical products.

Manufacturing Support

Provide recommendations on vaccine production techniques and scaling up manufacturing processes. Assist in establishing quality control measures and compliance with Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP).

Post-Marketing Surveillance

Develop strategies for monitoring vaccine safety and efficacy post-approval. Assist in collecting and analyzing data from post-marketing studies and adverse event reports.

Writing and Evaluating Manuscripts Support

Support in writing and evaluating manuscripts for publication in a peer-reviewed scientific journal, related to (pre)clinical, toxicological, and epidemiological studies, including writing overviews or reviews.

Educational and Training Programs

Offer training sessions for healthcare professionals and stakeholders on vaccine development trends and best practices. Provide educational resources and workshops focused on immunization strategies and public health implications.
